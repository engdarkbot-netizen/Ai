## Regulatory News

**New ETF Approval**: The Capital Market Authority approved the **"Al Rajhi MSCI Saudi Equity Dividends ETF"**, expanding the kingdom's exchange-traded fund offerings with a dividend-focused product from Al Rajhi Capital.

**Market Maker Designation**: Saudi Exchange Company approved **SNB Capital** as a Market Maker under the Exchange Traded Funds Market Making Framework, strengthening liquidity provision infrastructure for ETF trading.

## Market Context

**Recent Volatility**: The Sunday session represented a modest recovery after Wednesday's 0.42% decline (when TASI closed at 10,808.43).
