## Company News

### Aldrees Petroleum Posts Strong H1 Results

Aldrees Petroleum and Transport Services Co. reported an 18.41% increase in first-half net profit to **SR234.1 million** (vs SR197.7 million YoY), with revenue climbing 17.6% to SR14.16 billion. The company attributed growth to expansion of its service station network and truck fleet, along with higher sales from petroleum and transport divisions.

Q2 performance was even stronger: net profit jumped 28.13% year-on-year to SR123.9 million as revenue increased 18.01% to SR7.33 billion. Earnings per share rose to SR2.34 from SR1.98.

[Source: Arab News](https://www.arabnews.com/node/2651756/business-economy)

### Riyad Bank Launches SR5 Billion Sukuk Offering

Riyad Bank announced the launch of an offer and subscription period for Saudi riyal-denominated Additional Tier 1 Capital Sukuk under its public sukuk program. Key details:

- **Initial size:** SR5 billion (final amount TBD after offer closes)
- **Price per sukuk:** SR1,000
- **Fixed annual return:** 6.5% paid quarterly
- **Subscription period:** July 21 - August 3, 2026
- **Adviser/Arranger:** Riyad Capital

The bank's shares closed down 0.81% at SR20.90 following the announcement.
