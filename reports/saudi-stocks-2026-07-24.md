## Company News

### Earnings Reports

**Saudi Investment Bank (SAIB)** reported Q2 2026 net profit of **SAR 531.1mn**, up **3.6% YoY**, driven by increased fee and exchange income despite challenging market conditions. [Source: Tadawul via Al Rajhi Capital](https://alrajhi-capital.sa/-/media/Feature/AlRajhiCapital/ResearchListing/Daily-Reports/2026/july/DMR-English---23-July-2026.pdf)

**Electrical Industries Company (EIC)** delivered a standout performance with Q2 2026 net profit surging **54.7% YoY to SAR 211.5mn**, boosted by stronger demand across high-voltage, industrial, and oil & gas sectors—a clear beneficiary of ongoing Vision 2030 infrastructure spending. [Source: Tadawul via Al Rajhi Capital](https://alrajhi-capital.sa/-/media/Feature/AlRajhiCapital/ResearchListing/Daily-Reports/2026/july/DMR-English---23-July-2026.pdf)

### Major Contracts

**Saudi Steel Pipe (SSP)** signed a purchase order worth approximately **SAR 92.7mn with Saudi Aramco** to supply oil and gas coated steel pipes over a 10-month period, reinforcing its position as a key supplier to the kingdom's energy infrastructure. [Source: Tadawul via Al Rajhi Capital](https://alrajhi-capital.sa/-/media/Feature/AlRajhiCapital/ResearchListing/Daily-Reports/2026/july/DMR-English---23-July-2026.pdf)

### M&A Activity

**Saudi Re** completed its acquisition of a **22.5% equity stake in UK-based AdA Risk Holding Limited for GBP 8.95mn**, expanding its international reinsurance footprint and diversifying geographic exposure. [Source: Tadawul via Al Rajhi Capital](https://alrajhi-capital.sa/-/media/Feature/AlRajhiCapital/ResearchListing/Daily-Reports/2026/july/DMR-English---23-July-2026.pdf)
